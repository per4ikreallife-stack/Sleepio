The Concept

Sleepio isn't an app; it's a sleep ecosystem designed to automate your transition into deep rest.

Intelligent Limiter

Unlike standard timers, Sleepio uses a biological fade-out algorithm. It doesn't just stop; it mimics the natural descent into sleep.

Neural Silence

Once the threshold is reached, the system executes a total lock, ensuring no notifications or blue light disrupt your REM cycle.

Smart Reactivation

The system prepares your environment for the morning, slowly re-engaging services only when your body is ready to wake.

Zero Friction

One setting. Total automation. No menus, no ads, no distractions. Just the science of sleep.
